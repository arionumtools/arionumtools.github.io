ownership control

Share asset holders can make decisions through the arionum blockchain.


The voting results need to be confirmed by more than 50% of asset holders.

Any asset holder can create a proposal and provide aro address.
All accounts with the asset can send the minimum aro transaction to the relevant proposed account address.
Related aro income is owned by the proposer.
The longest voting time is one month and the shortest is no less than 10 minutes.
The proposer can confirm the end of the voting at any time.
The system will calculate the relevant asset holdings of all transfer accounts in the next block after the voting ends.
All accounts that send aro to this address own more than 50% of assets, and relevant proposals will become decisions
